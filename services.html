Services

2D Drafting

Detailed technical drawings for parts, assemblies, and fabrication. All drawings are tailored to manufacturing standards and can include dimensions, tolerances, material specs, and title blocks.

  • Part drawings
  • Assembly drawings
  • Shop/fabrication drawings
  • Exploded views
  • Drawing sets for production

3D Modeling

Parametric CAD models created for design visualization, prototyping, and manufacturing. Models can be exported to various formats for use in production, rendering, or simulation.

  • Mechanical components
  • Product assemblies
  • Concept-to-CAD modeling
  • Conversion from 2D to 3D
  • Export to STEP, IGES, STL, etc.

Drawing Conversion & Cleanup

Transform sketches, PDFs, or legacy drawings into clean, accurate CAD files. Ideal for updating old designs or preparing new documentation for manufacturing.

  • Hand sketch to CAD
  • PDF to DWG/DXF
  • Redrawing or cleanup of old files
  • Layer organization and standards compliance

Design Support & Collaboration

Flexible drafting services to support design teams, fabricators, and inventors. Ideal for early-stage development, prototyping, or getting ideas ready for quoting and production.

  • Design iteration support
  • Prototype-ready drawings
  • Manufacturer-ready documentation
  • Support across concept to production stages

File Standard & Formats:

  • File formats supported: DWG, DXF, SLDPRT, STEP, IGES, STL, etc.
  • Standards followed: ISO (metric). Imperial available upon request.
  • Remote collaboration: Available Canada-wide and internationally

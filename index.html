About

True Form Drafting delivers high-quality 2D drafting and 3D modeling services for mechanical design, prototyping, and fabrication. Based in Whistler, BC, the business supports clients across Canada and internationally, providing clear, build-ready technical drawings that streamline the path from idea to production. With a background in mechanical engineering and hands-on industry experience, True Form Drafting offers reliable support for both businesses and independent inventors. Learn more about me
